Rules for the local

Let's be honest — how many times did we spend ages looking for the 'fer à souder' or the 'colle à chaud'? 😅 That ends now. We want the local clean, organized, and we keep an eye on the material — so we finally get a good working atmosphere, unlike what we have today.

🧹

Clean

Leave the bench better than you found it. Clean as you go.

🗂️

Organized

Everything has its place — tools go back right after use.

👁️

Eye on the material

We track what we have and who's using it. Nothing goes missing.

  • Everything has its place 📍

    Each tool has a spot. The fer à souder and colle à chaud go back where they belong — no more 20-minute hunts.

  • Keep an eye on the material 🔧

    We track the inventory and who borrows what. The material is the club's most important asset — we protect it.

  • Clean as you go 🧼

    No leftover wires, no mess on the bench. Five minutes of cleanup keeps the local usable for everyone.

  • The local is everyone's 🤝

    Shared space, shared responsibility. Respect it and respect each other's work.

Simple rules, big difference. A clean, organized local = a good atmosphere and a club that actually gets things done. 🚀
