Our two big events

✈️ Aeronautics

Tunisian Aeroday

Tunisia's only national day fully dedicated to aeronautics — founded by Aerobotix INSAT. It turns aeronautics into a scientific, cultural and fun experience, bringing together students, startups, companies and enthusiasts around conferences, workshops and competitions.

  • Mission — popularize aeronautics for the public and uncover young talent.
  • AéroChallenge — pilot your drone through a predefined circuit.
  • AéroEntrepreneur — 24h to design and pitch a solution to a given problem.
  • CAD Challenge — 24h to build an innovative 3D model on SolidWorks / CATIA.
  • Novice Workshops — hands-on activities for young beginners (ages 6–17).
🤖 Robotics

Robolympix

One of the biggest annual robotics competitions in Tunisia, launched by Aerobotix INSAT in 2021. Every edition drops competitors into a new themed arena — pushing creativity, innovation and technical survival to the limit.

  • Scale — teams from all over Tunisia compete across junior and senior categories.
  • Themed arenas — each edition has its own world (e.g. Post-Apocalypse Arena).
  • The goal — stimulate innovation through real engineering and design challenges.
  • The vibe — high energy, real robots, real stakes. A showcase for Tunisian robotics.

These two events are the face of the club. We'll start preparing them early and put in serious work — because a strong event gives a strong image, and that's how sponsors, students and partners come to know Aerobotix. 🚀
