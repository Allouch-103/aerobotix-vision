The vision
Why this department?

The work is there... the voice isn't yet

Like every year, our problem is always the same: we build amazing, unique things — but nobody hears about them. And that hurts the club's reputation, and leaves us weak on the sponsoring side.
This year we made good progress on both of these axes — but we still want more. That's why the team in this department will be dedicated, with big impact, and we'll build on what we already have. 🚀
