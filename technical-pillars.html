✈️ Aeronautics

The axis we'll push much harder this year ✈️

  • Gliders & polyclubs 🛩️

    As usual, we'll keep taking part in gliders ('planneur') and the polyclubs events — staying present on the national aero scene.

  • RC drones 🚁

    A real, dedicated push on RC drones: building, piloting & a proper team behind them — not a side project anymore.

  • FPV & videography drones 🎥

    More focus on FPV / videography drones (FC) — flying, filming & capturing cinematic shots for the club and its events.

  • Equal footing ⚖️

    Aeronautics won't be the "second" axis anymore — same energy, same resources & the same ambition as robotics.
