🤖 Robotics · Eurobot 2026

Eurobot 2026

Aerobotix represented Tunisia at Eurobot 2026 in France — one of the world's most prestigious autonomous robotics competitions. As you all know, our team delivered an amazing performance, one of the best results in the club's history.

  • Top of the rankings — finishing among the very best teams in the world.
  • Ahead of established nations — France, Belgium, Spain, Greece, South Korea and more.
  • Real maturity — a result built on engineering, creativity and innovation.
  • We still want more 🔥 — and the next chapter starts with you.
📝 The form for the new Eurobot team is dropping very soon! Watch this space — applications open in the next few days. Your shot at the world stage starts here. 🚀
🏎️ Embedded · NXP Cup

NXP Cup

A global competition where student teams build, program and race autonomous model cars that follow a track using a camera — pure embedded systems and control, with industry-standard tools.

  • Worldwide scale — 250+ teams compete each season across continents.
  • Many teams, as usual — Aerobotix will once again field several teams.
  • Real industry skills — microcontrollers, sensors, motor control and computer vision.
  • A proven track record — a competition we keep coming back to, stronger every year.
